ABOUT

I'm a product manager who came up through data analytics — which means I think in systems, care about instrumentation, and don't trust a metric I can't trace back to a source. My approach to product is rooted in domain depth: I don't parachute into a market and guess. I work to understand the actual problem before I reach for a solution.

My career has cut across fintech, content intelligence, government policy, and now AI + motorsport — industries that seem unrelated but share a thread: high-stakes decisions, fragmented data, and stakeholders who don't always agree on the problem. That's the PM work I find genuinely interesting.

Right now I lead a cross-functional team of 6 at Race Engineering — building the intelligence layer that motorsport teams don't have yet. Two patents are under process. The work is new, and that's why I'm doing it.
