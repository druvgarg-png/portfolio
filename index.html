About

I'm a product manager who came up through data analytics. I think in systems, care about instrumentation, and don't trust a metric I can't trace back to a source. Domain depth first, solutions second.

My career has moved through fintech, government policy, content intelligence, and now GPU cloud infrastructure. Different industries, same thread: high-stakes decisions, fragmented data, and stakeholders who don't agree on the problem.
